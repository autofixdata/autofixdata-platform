<h2>What You Actually Get With ALLDATA Repair</h2>

<p>ALLDATA Repair is the product most independent workshops are looking for. Here's what's genuinely included:</p>

<h3>OEM Repair Procedures</h3>
<p>ALLDATA holds direct data licensing agreements with OEMs including Ford, GM, Stellantis, Honda, Toyota, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Volkswagen Group, and many more. Repair procedures are sourced directly from manufacturer technical information systems — the same documentation franchised dealers use.</p>

<h3>Wiring Diagrams</h3>
<p>Full electrical circuit diagrams in OEM monochrome factory style. Includes component location diagrams, connector views, and pin-out information. Accurate and comprehensive for US-spec vehicles. European-spec data is present but thinner than dedicated European platforms.</p>

<h3>Technical Service Bulletins (TSBs)</h3>
<p>ALLDATA's TSB database is one of the most comprehensive in the industry. TSBs are indexed by fault code, symptom, and component — making it fast to check whether a known manufacturer fix exists before starting a diagnostic sequence. This alone prevents unnecessary part replacements and repeat visits.</p>

<h3>Recall Information</h3>
<p>ALLDATA includes recall data integrated with the <a href="https://www.nhtsa.gov/recalls" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">official US vehicle recall database</a>, flagging any open recalls on the vehicle being accessed.</p>

<h3>What ALLDATA Does NOT Include</h3>
<ul>
<li>Real-world community confirmed fixes (no equivalent to Mitchell1's SureTrack)</li>
<li>Best-in-class ADAS calibration procedure depth (AutoData leads here)</li>
<li>Colour wiring diagrams (diagrams are OEM monochrome — prefer HaynesPro for colour)</li>
<li>Full coverage of European-only vehicle variants without a supplementary European platform</li>
</ul>
