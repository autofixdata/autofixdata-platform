<h2>How to Access ALLDATA Login</h2>

<h3>Step 1: Navigate to the Login Portal</h3>
<p>Open your browser and go to <strong>my.alldata.com</strong>. This is the primary portal for all ALLDATA Repair and ALLDATA Collision subscribers. Bookmark this URL — the ALLDATA main website (alldata.com) is for sales and marketing, not for product access.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Enter Your Credentials</h3>
<p>Enter the username and password provided by your ALLDATA sales representative or account administrator. If your workshop has a multi-user licence, each technician should have individual credentials — do not share a single login across multiple workstations, as ALLDATA monitors concurrent sessions and may lock accounts for terms-of-service violations.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Select Your Product</h3>
<p>After login, you'll see a dashboard with your active subscriptions. Click on ALLDATA Repair to access repair data, or ALLDATA Collision if you have a collision-specific subscription.</p>

<h2>Setting Up Multi-User Access</h2>

<p>For workshops with multiple technicians, ALLDATA offers multi-user licences that support 3–5 concurrent sessions. Here's how to set up multi-user access:</p>

<ol>
<li><strong>Contact ALLDATA sales</strong> — Upgrade your current single-user licence to a multi-user tier. Pricing increases to approximately £160–£200/month for 3–5 concurrent users.</li>
<li><strong>Create individual accounts</strong> — Your account administrator can create separate usernames for each technician through the admin panel at my.alldata.com.</li>
<li><strong>Assign permissions</strong> — Set appropriate access levels for each user. Not all technicians need admin-level access to billing and subscription management.</li>
<li><strong>Test on each workstation</strong> — Verify that each technician can log in simultaneously from their workshop bay or office computer.</li>
</ol>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What is the ALLDATA login URL?</h3>
<p>The official login URL is <a href="https://my.alldata.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">my.alldata.com</a>. Do not use third-party links or search engine ads — always navigate directly to the official URL to avoid phishing attempts.</p>

<h3>How do I reset my ALLDATA password?</h3>
<p>Click "Forgot Password" on the login page at my.alldata.com. Enter the email address associated with your account. ALLDATA will send a password reset link. If you don't receive it within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact ALLDATA support directly.</p>

<h3>Can I use ALLDATA on multiple devices?</h3>
<p>With a single-user licence, you can use ALLDATA on any device — but only one session at a time. If you log in from a second device, your first session will be terminated. Multi-user licences allow 3–5 simultaneous sessions.</p>
