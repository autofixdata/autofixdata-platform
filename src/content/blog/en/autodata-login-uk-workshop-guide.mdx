<h2>Common AutoData Login Issues & Fixes</h2>

<h3>1. "Maximum users reached" Error</h3>
<p>If your UK garage has an AutoData license with limited concurrent users (e.g., 2 users), and a third mechanic tries to log in, this error appears. <strong>The fix:</strong> Ask a colleague to log out properly using the "Sign Out" button, rather than just closing the browser tab. Closing the tab can sometimes leave the session "hanging" for several minutes.</p>

<h3>2. Forgotten Password</h3>
<p>Click the "Forgotten Password?" link on the login page. An email will be sent to the administrator's email address. If the email doesn't arrive within 5 minutes, check your Junk/Spam folder.</p>

<h3>3. Blank Screen After Login</h3>
<p>This is almost always a browser cache issue. If you hit <code>CTRL + F5</code> on your keyboard, it forces a hard refresh. If that fails, clear your browser history/cache or try logging in via "Incognito" or "Private Browsing" mode.</p>
