<h3>Troubleshooting the ProDemand Login</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>"Maximum User Limit Reached":</strong> Mitchell1 strictly monitors concurrent logins. If you pay for a single-user license, attempting to log in on a bay laptop while the service writer is logged in at the front desk will result in a kick-out. You must either upgrade your seat count or ensure others log out properly.</li>
<li><strong>Browser Compatibility:</strong> ProDemand relies heavily on PDF viewers for wiring diagrams. Ensure you are using an updated version of Google Chrome or Edge. Clear your cache if diagrams refuse to load.</li>
</ul>
